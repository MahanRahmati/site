Taal

Website

Taal is an on-demand electric scooter sharing platform that provides an eco-friendly solution for urban transportation. Through the mobile app, users can instantly locate nearby scooters, unlock them via QR scan, and complete their journey with cashless payments. By offering zero-emission micromobility, Taal helps reduce urban traffic congestion and air pollution while providing a faster, more efficient way to navigate the city compared to traditional transportation methods.
